Custom Editor Features

To make my components easy to work with, I set about customising the Unity Editor. At the time, I had never tried this before. I now understand it to be an essential part of the development process. You are not just using Unity to build your game, you are building upon Unity to create the engine which will make your game possible. The work is tough but satisfying I think.
